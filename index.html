About Us

Intech Insurance Surveyors And Loss Assessors Pvt. Limited is licensed by the Insurance Regulatory Development Authority [IRDA] vide No SLA No 72454 for a survey of all the departments of the General Insurance Industry. Intech, as India's most preferred and trusted Insurance Survey and Loss Assessment Firm has always been at the forefront in providing a wide gamut of loss adjusting and claims management services across various disciplines and for the clients across India. We have crafted for ourselves an unwavering position in the market as the leaders and delivering topnotch professional services.

We, at Intech Insurance Surveyors and Loss Assessors, have a big team with experienced and qualified loss adjusters, Technical staff, Surveyors, Engineers, Management specialists, Accountants, and Economists. We always ensure a quick response with our reliable approach, as deemed fit in all scenarios that include but not just limited to natural disasters, adversities or calamities. Read More..
