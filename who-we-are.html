Who We Are

About Us

INTECH Insurance Surveyors And Loss Assessors Pvt. Limited is licensed by the Insurance Regulatory Development Authority of India [IRDAI] vide No SLA No 72454 to carry out the survey and loss assessment of all departments of the General Insurance Industry.
INTECH, as India's most preferred and trusted Insurance Survey and Loss Assessment company has always been at the forefront in providing a wide gamut of loss adjusting and claims management services across various disciplines across India. INTECH crafted by itself an unwavering position in the market as the leaders and delivering topnotch professional services.
INTECH team consists of various diversified professionals not limited to qualified loss adjustors but also Technical staff, Civil, Mechanical and Electrical Engineers, Management specialists, Accountants, and Economists.
INTECH ensure a quick response with reliable approach, as deemed fit in all scenarios that include but not just limited to natural disasters, adversities or calamities.


Delivering value-added services with all candor, integrity, and transparency for we believe in serving with all ethicality and heartitude.

You can count on us for comprehensive, all-inclusive, value-added services such as claims assistance and claim management in virtually all of the major domains of insurance such as Engineering, Marine, Fire, Motor, LOP and other Miscellaneous cases.
INTECH has qualified professionals equipped to deliver other ancillary services associated with the insurance domains, such as Risk Inspections, Valuations, Pre-Dispatches and Chartered Engineering Certifications.
INTECH always put to use avant-garde strategies in order to ensure that it always do the best for the clients, and of course, in compliance with the ever-increasing demands of the market. The team adopted a more flexible and goal-oriented approach to claim surveying losses, claims management, as required to comply with the standards in the industry.
INTECH forte lies in providing more reliable, fast-track, unequivocal and innovative services, putting to use advanced technology and with more focus on client communication, responsiveness and tangible outcomes.
